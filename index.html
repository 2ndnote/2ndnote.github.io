Our Mission

Our mission is to broker public school teachersâ€™ classroom needs to those with school and office surplus in an "earth-friendly" manner. Last year, we collected and cleaned close to 500 recorder instruments and provided them to three elementary schools in East Palo Alto. We also created a website for teachers who are seeking support, and match them with volunteers who employ various charitable vehicles to acquire the requested items. Volunteers committed to help the teachers in the system will earn volunteer service hours. Anyone can volunteer!

About Us

Cynthia Liu

Founder

Cynthia is responsible for communicating with donors, sponsors, and school administrators. She also does donation collections and distributions, as well as volunteer recruiting.

Cynthia is currently a senior at Palo Alto High School. She is lots of experience with teaching and supporting teachers-in-need: she was an intern at the Boys and Girls Club of the Peninsula (Brentwood site), a teacher for Connexpedition (a program which allowed her to teach impoverished children English in New Taipei), and a counselor at the Wisdom Culture Education Organization summer camp.

Emily Yu

Founder, Web

Emily is responsible for developing and maintaining the website for 2ndNote. She also recruits volunteers, does donor outreach, donation collections and distributions, as well as web communications.

Emily has various experience with coding, demonstrating mastery in Swift, Python, Javascript, Ruby, and HTML/CSS, and has released apps on the iOS app store. Emily is currently a senior at Palo Alto High School. She was a member of the school Science Olympiad team and coached the Jordan Middle School's Science Olympiad Club.
